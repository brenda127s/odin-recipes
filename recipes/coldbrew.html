Cold Brew Recipe

How to make cold brew coffee at home!

You may have consumed cold brewed coffee at a nearby cafe or purchased it in ready-to-go containers at the store for ultimate convenience. Yet, once you discover how effortlessly you can draft it at home, you'll ponder why you haven't been brewing it yourself all this time.

What is cold brew coffee

Cold brew coffee remians cool throughout its preparation. Unlike the quick-brewing methods taking just a few minutes, cold brew involves a slow 12 to 15 hour steeping process. This results in a less acidic and bitter coffee, offering a sweeter, fuller-bodied flavor that truly excels when it comes to iced coffee.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Coffee beans
  • Milk or creamer (optional)
  • Ice

Steps to make cold brew coffee

  1. Grind coffee beans to a medium roast consistency.
  2. Add the ground coffee beans and four cups of water to a glass storage container.
  3. Steep the coffee for 12 to 15 hours.
  4. Strain the coffee through a fine mesh sieve or nut milk bag
  5. Pour the strained cold brew into a jar or other container and refrigerate.
  6. Enjoy the cold brew over ice or add milk/creamer.
