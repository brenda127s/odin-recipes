Latte Recipe

Craft a refreshing and creamy iced latte at home!

What could be more revitalizing than a chilled latte? It's no surprise that this espresso beverage enjoys widespread popularity. The harmonious blend of robust coffee, subtle sweetness, and velvety frothed milk is truly delightful. You don't have to rush to your preferred coffee spot for this treat, it's a breeze to whip up at home! There's nothing more satisfying than an iced latte on warm summer afternoons (or any time, for that matter).

What exactly is an iced latte?

An iced latte compromises espresso, milk, and a sweetener if desired. The milk is typically frothed to replicate the steamed milk found in a regular latte. Now, how does it differ from iced coffee? Iced coffee is made with brewed coffee and can include milk if desired. The key distinction lies in the use of espresso in the iced latte, giving it a bolder, richer flavor.

Ingredients

  • Espresso roast coffee
  • Whole milk
  • Maple syrup
  • Ice

Steps to make an iced latte

  1. Make a double shot of espresso with the preparation method of your choice (espresso machine, french press, etc).
  2. Froth 1/4 cup of whole milk either by whisking until it is frothy or shaking it in a covered jar/container.
  3. Pour the espresso and 1 teaspoon maple syrup over ice.
  4. Add milk and serve.
