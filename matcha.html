Matcha Recipe

Create a delicous and rich iced matcha latte!

Indulge in the richness of this iced matcha latte, a creamy and energizing drink that can be enjoyed either hot or cold. If you're yearning for the familiar taste of an iced matcha latte from your local coffee spot, this recipe is tailor-made for you, allowing you to recreate your beloved beverage in the comfort of your own home.

What is matcha?

Before getting into the recipe, let me provide some context on matcha. Originating in Japan during the 110s, this green tea powder plays a central role in traditional Japanese tea cerimonies. Crafted from shade-grown green tea leaves finely ground into powder, matcha is reowned for its abundant antioxidants. When consumed, it delivers a higher caffeine content compared to regular green tea, usually less than a typical cup of coffee, as you injest the entire tea leaf.

Ingredients

  • Matcha powder
  • Hot water
  • Milk
  • Simple syrup
  • Ice

Steps to make an iced matcha latte

  1. Add ice, milk, and simple syrup into a glass cup.
  2. Whisk matcha and hot water until there are no lumps. It should be smooth and foamy.
  3. Transfer it into the fridge for about 15 minutes to cool off.
  4. Pour the matcha mixture into the milk glass and enjoy.
